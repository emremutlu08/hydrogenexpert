# Competitor Monitoring Cadence

Status: Active
Last updated: 2026-06-17
Owner: Agent
Source of truth: `competitor-profiles.md`, current public competitor pages, HydrogenExpert production pages

## Purpose

Keep the Liquid and Hydrogen competitor map useful after this implementation without turning it into stale one-time research.

## Quarterly Refresh

- Recheck US and UK SERPs for Shopify Liquid agency, Shopify Hydrogen agency, headless Shopify agency, Liquid to Hydrogen migration, and Shopify Hydrogen developer terms.
- Revisit all 22 competitor profile pages and note changes in Liquid, Hydrogen, headless, migration, SEO, and agency positioning.
- Refresh traffic-priority assumptions using available public indicators or third-party estimates when accessible.
- Compare HydrogenExpert priority pages against competitor messaging: `/shopify-hydrogen-vs-liquid`, `/liquid-to-hydrogen-migration`, `/shopify-hydrogen-packages`, `/shopify-hydrogen-fit-audit`, `/headless-shopify-agency`, `/services`, and `/case-studies`.
- Flag any new competitor pattern that affects Liquid cleanup, migration SEO, maintenance cost, agency comparison, or integration boundaries.

## Output

- Update `competitor-profiles.md` only when the competitor position actually changed.
- Add small backlog tasks to `task-backlog.md` instead of broad redesign requests.
- Do not publish new proof, logos, metrics, partner claims, or office claims unless independently approved.
