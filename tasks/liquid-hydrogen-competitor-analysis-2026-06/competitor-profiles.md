# Liquid and Hydrogen Competitor Profiles

Status: Active
Last updated: 2026-06-17
Owner: Agent
Source of truth: Public competitor pages, public traffic tools, HydrogenExpert repo, Agent analysis

## Purpose

This file reviews each competitor one by one before any HydrogenExpert implementation work. It turns public competitor evidence into actionable positioning, content, proof, and conversion opportunities for HydrogenExpert.

## Analysis Boundaries

- Focus markets: United States and United Kingdom first.
- Focus buyer problem: Shopify merchants deciding between Liquid improvement, Liquid-to-Hydrogen migration, custom Hydrogen, broader headless, or no rebuild.
- HydrogenExpert frame: senior-led Shopify Hydrogen services, fixed-scope builds from `$2K-$5K` when appropriate, and honest Liquid/no-rebuild guidance.
- Traffic data: third-party estimates are directional, not first-party analytics.
- Proof safety: do not copy competitor claims, invent partner badges, invent metrics, or imply HydrogenExpert has agency scale it cannot prove.

## Scoring Dimensions

| Dimension | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Strategic fit | How close the competitor is to HydrogenExpert's desired buyer and offer. |
| Liquid strength | How clearly the competitor wins Liquid/theme-native Shopify work. |
| Hydrogen/headless strength | How clearly the competitor wins Hydrogen or headless Shopify work. |
| Trust strength | Public proof, partner status, case studies, and visible market authority. |
| Traffic strength | Directional public visibility based on traffic tools, search footprint, and content depth. |

## 1. Limely

Source links: [Hydrogen page](https://www.limely.co.uk/shopify/hydrogen), [Shopify development](https://www.limely.co.uk/shopify/development), [Shopify agency](https://www.limely.co.uk/shopify)

Strategic fit: Very high. Limely is one of the clearest matches because it explicitly positions Hydrogen as powerful but not right for every store. Its Hydrogen page says Hydrogen adds complexity and cost compared with a well-built Liquid theme, which is very close to HydrogenExpert's honest recommendation style.

What they do well:
- Separates bespoke Liquid theme development from Hydrogen/headless work.
- Uses decision language instead of only "headless is faster" hype.
- Shows trust with Google/Trustpilot signals and case-study style performance outcomes.
- Sells UK-local ecommerce agency confidence while still being technology-specific.

Target buyer:
- Ambitious UK brands already considering Shopify, Shopify Plus, Magento, Hyva, or platform migration.
- Buyers who want an agency to design, build, support, and grow the store.

Compared with HydrogenExpert:
- They are broader and more agency-like.
- HydrogenExpert can beat them on direct senior ownership, fixed-scope budget clarity, and narrower Liquid-to-Hydrogen judgment.
- They currently beat HydrogenExpert on public social proof, local UK agency reassurance, and visible Liquid offer depth.

Task seeds:
- Build a dedicated "Liquid vs Hydrogen fit review" page with direct senior-opinion framing.
- Make Liquid cleanup visible as a real recommendation, not only a fallback.
- Add a short "when I recommend Liquid instead" proof block to major commercial pages.
