- Make "Liquid cleanup or no rebuild" visible on every major decision route, not hidden as a fallback.
- Keep `/liquid-to-hydrogen-migration` focused on migration risk: routes, redirects, canonicals, schema, analytics, checkout, and rollback checks.
- Use `/shopify-hydrogen-fit-audit` as the main competitor counter to large agency discovery calls.
- Separate Liquid proof from Hydrogen proof so case studies are honest and easier to map to buyer decisions.
- Add more public proof over time through case studies, source-grounded articles, and off-site authority rather than fake partner-scale claims.
- Keep US/UK trust practical: timezone overlap, async scoping, direct senior ownership, and clear scope boundaries.
- Treat integration-heavy stores as custom scope early so fixed `$2K-$5K` packages stay credible.
