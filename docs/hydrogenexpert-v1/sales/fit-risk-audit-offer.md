# Hydrogen Fit & Risk Audit Offer

## Positioning

The Hydrogen Fit & Risk Audit is a paid decision memo for Shopify Plus and growth-stage brands considering Hydrogen, headless Shopify, or a custom storefront rebuild.

It is designed to prevent the wrong expensive scope.

## Range

- Price range: $1.5K-$5K
- Timeline: 5-10 business days
- Format: written memo + Loom walkthrough

## Output

The audit answers:

- Should the brand stay on Liquid?
- Should Liquid be refactored first?
- Would focused optimization solve the pressure?
- Is Hydrogen commercially justified?
- Should the rebuild be delayed?
- What is the safest next scope?

## What Is Included

- Storefront context review
- Liquid constraint analysis
- Hydrogen fit analysis
- SEO, route, canonical, and metadata risk
- Performance, SSR, and data loading risk
- Analytics, consent, and tracking risk
- App stack and integration risk
- Maintenance readiness
- Budget and timeline range
- Recommended next step

## What Is Not Included

- Full redesign
- Full implementation
- Analytics rebuild
- App migration
- Guaranteed SEO, conversion, or revenue lift
- A default recommendation to use Hydrogen
