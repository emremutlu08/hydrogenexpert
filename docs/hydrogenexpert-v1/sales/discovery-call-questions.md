# Discovery Call Questions

## Storefront Context

| Question | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| What is the current store URL and storefront stack? | Confirms Liquid, Hydrogen, or another headless setup. |
| What changed commercially that made Hydrogen come up now? | Separates real pressure from tech curiosity. |
| What parts of the storefront are hardest to change today? | Finds theme, app, data, or team constraints. |
| What is the current monthly revenue band and Shopify Plus status? | Checks proportionality of custom storefront spend. |

## Risk Areas

| Question | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Which routes drive organic traffic today? | Protects SEO and migration planning. |
| Are product descriptions, variants, and collection content visible in initial HTML? | Checks crawl and rendering risk. |
| What apps are critical to PDP, cart, search, reviews, subscriptions, loyalty, or analytics? | Identifies integration risk. |
| What analytics and consent setup must survive launch? | Prevents measurement loss. |

## Buying Motion

| Question | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Is the team looking for audit, migration, build, optimization, or retainer support? | Maps to the service path. |
| What budget range is realistic before design or implementation starts? | Avoids mismatched proposals. |
| What timeline pressure exists? | Surfaces rescue risk versus planned work. |
| Who will maintain the storefront after launch? | Checks Hydrogen operating readiness. |

## Close

Ask for access or artifacts only after fit is clear: current theme/repo notes, analytics/GSC screenshots, route list, app list, and top product/collection URLs.
