# HydrogenExpert Positioning Rules

Last updated: 2026-05-05

## Decision

HydrogenExpert should be positioned as a senior-led Shopify Hydrogen services asset and agency alternative for Shopify Plus and growth-stage brands.

It should not pretend to be a traditional US office, broad Shopify agency, paid-media agency, creative agency, or multi-person delivery company unless that proof exists publicly and operationally.

## Approved Phrases

- Senior-led Shopify Hydrogen services
- Shopify Hydrogen agency alternative
- Senior-led Hydrogen studio
- Direct senior Hydrogen delivery
- Hydrogen strategy, audit, migration, development, optimization, and support
- Shopify Plus and growth-stage ecommerce brands
- Remote senior Hydrogen support for US/global teams

## Phrases To Avoid Or Use Carefully

- Full-service Shopify agency
- US Shopify agency
- Local US office
- Agency team
- Shopify Plus Partner
- Certified Shopify Partner
- Best Shopify agency
- Guaranteed SEO results
- Hydrogen is always better than Liquid

## Page-Level Rules

- Homepage: lead with senior-led Hydrogen services and agency-alternative positioning.
- `/shopify-hydrogen-agency`: own "Shopify Hydrogen agency" intent while explaining the honest agency-alternative model.
- `/shopify-hydrogen-agency-usa`: speak to US buyers without claiming local presence.
- `/shopify-hydrogen-developer`: keep developer intent for buyers who want direct implementation.
- `/custom-shopify-hydrogen-storefront`: own custom Hydrogen development/build intent.
- `/shopify-hydrogen-audit`: make the first paid diagnostic clear.
- Blog posts: use technical depth to route readers to relevant service pages.

## Proof Rules

- Use only approved public proof: Upwork Top Rated Plus, 100% JSS, Upwork hours, LinkedIn following, Udemy course, approved case studies, and public Rebel Bunny feedback.
- Do not invent testimonials, screenshots, logos, revenue, conversion lifts, partner badges, office locations, or team claims.
- If a claim is not approved or public, convert it into a qualitative constraint instead of a metric.

## Source-Grounding Rules

- Shopify, Hydrogen, Oxygen, Storefront API, React Router, SEO, analytics, and deployment claims must be grounded in official Shopify docs checked through the Shopify developer docs search flow.
- Commercial guidance should separate official Shopify facts from Emre's operator interpretation.
- Every major buyer-facing page should keep a clear "when Hydrogen is wrong" path.
